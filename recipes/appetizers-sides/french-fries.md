French Fries
==========

## Ingredients
- some potatoes, idk lol
- Ice water
- Peanut oil

## Instructions
1. Wash potatoes
2. Cut potatoes
3. Place potatoes in water for 20 minutes.
4. Boil potatoes for 5 minutes at 300f
5. Freeze potatoes
6. When you are ready thaw potatoes
7. When potatoes are thawed boil for 2 minutes at 375f
